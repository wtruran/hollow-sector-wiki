Commodities

The 19 trade goods that fill freight holds — and the loot you take when you raid one.

Commodities are manufactured and consumer goods, distinct from the raw materials you mine. Every freight contract is hauling one of them, and a freighter that runs a raid or recovers salvage brings commodities home too. They are storable, stackable, and sell on the same market as mined goods.

A commodity's rating is paired to job ratings: higher-rated contracts haul more valuable goods. Raid an A-rated convoy and you're carrying off Quantum Processors; raid an F-rated one and it's Waste Containers.

Like all goods, commodity market prices fluctuate ±30% from base value on a 6-hour refresh, and selling large volumes of the same item at one station depresses its price until the daily reset. Spread big hauls across stations.

Where commodities come from

  • Freight contracts — the goods you're paid to deliver (you don't keep these; they're handed over at the dropoff).
  • Piracy — raid an NPC freight fleet and seize whatever it was carrying.
  • Salvage — drifting wreck-cargo is a mix of ore and commodities.
