Stations

All 50 space stations in the sector — location, orbit, and home eligibility.

Station positions are dynamic — every station orbits its parent body in real time. Coordinates shown on the in-game map update continuously. All planet-orbiting stations are eligible as a home base. Sun-orbiting stations (belt and waypoint stations) are not eligible for home selection but can host purchased ship yards.

These stations orbit one of the six Kepler-11 planets. They complete one orbit per real day and are eligible as your home base at game start.
