Asteroids

All asteroid fields in the sector — location, materials, and risk tier.

Risk values shown are the range for each asteroid based on its highest-tier material. The actual risk re-rolls every 4 hours — check the asteroid popup on the map for the current live value. Asteroid positions are dynamic; all planet asteroids complete one orbit per real day, belt asteroids per 8 real days.
