Introduction

Everything you need to run cargo, mine asteroids, and survive the void.

Corporations abandoned Hollow Sector years ago. Now independent haulers and mercenaries risk the void to keep trade alive. You are one of them.

What is Hollow Sector?

Hollow Sector is a passive idle trading game played as a Chrome extension. You build a fleet of freight, fighter, and mining ships, take contracts from the job board, mine asteroids for raw materials, and sell your haul on fluctuating markets — all while the game ticks along in the background.

Ships travel in real time. A contract that crosses 100MM of space on a slow freighter takes real minutes. You don't click to move — you assign, confirm, and wait. Check back when your fleet lands.

Core Loop

1. Buy Ships
Start with ƒ15,000. Purchase freight, fighter, or mining ships from the vendor. A limited ship rotates into stock every 2 hours.
2. Take Contracts
The job board offers freight and defense contracts. Assign ships and dispatch. The board refreshes every 4 hours.
3. Mine Asteroids
Send mining ships to asteroids on the map. Choose materials, assign escorts, and wait for the return trip.
4. Sell & Expand
Sell mined materials at market rates. Prices shift every 6 hours. Reinvest profits into fleet upgrades, ship yards, and station improvements like the Refinery and Drone Bay.
