Market

Selling mined materials, supply depression, and the price refresh cycle.

Station Modal Tabs

Opening a home base or owned ship yard shows a secondary tab row: Storage | Market | Shop | Upgrades.

  • Storage — view and sell your inventory, and trigger refinery and production runs if upgrades are installed.
  • Market — sell goods and view current prices for all raw materials, refined materials, and commodities at this station. Prices fluctuate on the 6-hour refresh cycle.
  • Shop — buy Fresh Produce, Food Rations, Medical Supplies, Recycled Materials, and Waste Containers directly into local storage at the current market price. No freighter needed.
  • Upgrades — purchase and manage station upgrades (Refinery, Production Array, Drone Bay, and others).

Selling Materials

Mined materials are stored at your home base (or the nearest owned ship yard) automatically when a mining run completes. Open a station and go to the Market tab to sell materials at the current market rate.

Market Prices

Prices are randomised per station within a 70%–130% range of each material's base value. A ƒ5,000/CU rare material will sell for anywhere between ƒ3,500 and ƒ6,500 depending on which station you sell at.

Prices refresh every 6 hours. The countdown is shown on the Market Rates tab. If the timer reaches zero while the screen is open, prices update automatically.

The Market Rates tab displays two sections: Raw Materials and Refined Materials, each showing the current live price at that station.

Supply Depression

Each time you sell a material at a station, its effective price drops. Every 10 SU sold reduces the price by 10% at that station. At 100 SU sold, the price reaches zero.

Don't flood one station. Sell large hauls across multiple stations or wait for the daily sales volume reset at midnight. The reset clears all supply depression, restoring full prices.

Ship Yards & Transfer Runs

Once you own a ship yard at a second station (see Ship Yards), you can transfer materials between stations using freight ships. This lets you move inventory to a station with higher prices or less supply depression.

Transfer runs also carry risk. The risk is based on the total value of the transferred cargo — higher value loads attract more pirate attention.
