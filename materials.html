Refined Materials

Refined materials are produced at stations with a Refinery upgrade (see Station Upgrades). Each raw material has exactly one refined product. Refined goods sell for more than their raw source and fluctuate on the same 70%–130% market cycle.

Refining costs and time. Refine cost is 10% of the raw material's base value per SU. Refine time per SU: Common = 6 real seconds, Uncommon = 12 real seconds, Rare = 18 real seconds. The quantity selector in the Storage tab shows total cost and time before you confirm.

Produced Materials

Some materials aren't mined from asteroids at all — they're produced by a faction ship and delivered to your stations, where they sell on the same market cycle as everything else.

MaterialSourceBase Value (ƒ/CU)Piracy Risk
EnergyThe Perihelion (1 SU / hour)3,500none
Energy is delivered to your nearest station by the Perihelion's tender. It sells like a premium commodity and is also spent to buy faction ships that require it (the Synodos needs 10 SU, the Custodian 25 SU).

Notes

Risk ranges are per-asteroid, not per-material. An asteroid's risk is set to the highest range of any material it contains, then a specific value is rolled within that range every 4 hours.
Market prices fluctuate ±30% from base values on a 6-hour refresh cycle — for both raw and refined materials. Check your station's Market Rates tab before selling.
Supply depression reduces the effective sell price when you sell large volumes at the same station. Spread sales across stations or wait for the daily reset to maximise income.
