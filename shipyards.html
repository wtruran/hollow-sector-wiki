Ship Yards

Expanding your network — purchasing yards and upgrading stations.

What is a Ship Yard?

A ship yard is a facility you own at a station. Your starting home base comes with one. Additional yards can be purchased at any other station for ƒ200,000.

Owning a yard at a station lets you:

  • Store mined materials there (ships can return to any owned yard)
  • Sell materials at that station's market prices
  • Dispatch transfer runs to move inventory between yards
  • Access that station's local market rates
  • Purchase station upgrades (Refinery, Drone Bay)

Purchasing a Yard

On the Map, tap any station you don't already own. If you have at least ƒ200,000 and no outstanding lien, the "Purchase Ship Yard" button will be active. The purchase is instant.

You must have zero lien to purchase a yard. Pay off any outstanding insurance debt first.

Transfer Runs

Once you have two or more yards, you can transfer materials between them. Open the home base or a yard station and select the transfer option. Assign a freight ship to carry the cargo.

Transfer runs carry piracy risk based on cargo value:

  • ƒ0–15,000 in cargo → Risk 0
  • Increases by 1 per ƒ15,000 of cargo value
  • Capped at Risk 7

Assign escorts accordingly. A fully laden rare-material transfer is worth assigning a strong fighter.

Station Upgrades

Your home base and any owned ship yard can be upgraded from the station modal's Upgrades tab. Two upgrades are available:

Refinery — ƒ50,000

A Refinery converts raw materials in storage into refined goods, which sell at higher base values. Each raw material has one refined product (see Materials for the full list).

  • To use: go to the station's Storage tab, select a raw material, choose a quantity, and confirm. The quantity selector shows the total refine cost and estimated time.
  • Cost: 10% of the raw material's base value per SU refined.
  • Refine time per SU: Common = 6 real seconds, Uncommon = 12 real seconds, Rare = 18 real seconds.
  • Each station's Refinery operates independently — you can run refineries at multiple yards simultaneously.

Drone Bay — ƒ100,000

A Drone Bay deploys an autonomous fleet of mining drones from the station. Drones require no player-owned ship and bypass all piracy risk entirely.

  • Setup: from the station's Upgrades tab, select a target asteroid and which material to collect. Drones loop continuously until you stop them.
  • Drones: cost ƒ5,000 each, maximum 10 per bay. Each drone carries 1 SU per trip.
  • Range: 500 WU — drones can only reach asteroids within 500 WU of their station.
  • Speed: 30 real minutes per 100 WU of travel.
  • Piracy: drones ignore all piracy risk — no escorts needed.
  • Each Drone Bay is per-station and independent of other bays or your player fleet.
On the map, active drones appear as a purple circle traveling between their station and the target asteroid, with a faint dashed purple route line showing their path.

Strategy

Multiple ship yards open up arbitrage opportunities. Market prices vary by station — a material at 80% of base at your home station might be at 125% at a distant yard. Transfer the inventory and pocket the difference.

Installing a Refinery before selling rare hauls is high-value: converting Rhodium (ƒ5,000/CU) to Rhodium Catalyst (ƒ6,000/CU) is a 20% gain minus the small refining fee.

Drone Bays are best placed at stations close to rich common-material asteroids — they generate a steady low-maintenance income stream without committing any player ships.
